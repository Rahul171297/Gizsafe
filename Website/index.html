The all-in-one home management app

Home is where your heart is. We created Gizsafe (Gizmo/Gadget Safe) for your heart and peace of mind. Effortless and optimum management of your home (gizmos/gadgets/appliances), be it routine operation, registration, service (scheduled and non-scheduled) or troubleshooting.

Gizsafe will maintain your Gizmo library for you, guide you on optimum usage, help you in fixing them.

Home organization isn’t one size fits all.

Neither is Centriq. Just snap a picture of the appliances, electronics, and tools in your home. We’ll add the user manuals, warranties, how-to-videos and more. Personalize and group all your information to make it as unique as your home.

Your aide-de-camp.

You already have a lot on your hands. Let us offload your home management part from your busy shoulders. Just upload pics/invoice of your Gizmos. We will maintain their warranty and service records, provide their user manuals and how-to-do videos/tutorials and provide information on service centres, spares and accessories.

Our Features

Manuals

We add and retain the user manuals of your Gizmos as ready reference. Say ‘bye bye’ to retaining and searching through hard copies.

Supplies

You can process or track your requests for service or maintenance of your products. We will raise such requests on your behalf with the brands/service providers, track them and assist you till they are closed to your satisfaction.

How To Videos

We will search and add the How to Videos for your Gizmos sparing you the trouble of navigating through the internet junk.

Asset Registration

We will help you in effortless registration of your prized possessions and auto populate warranty details for you.
